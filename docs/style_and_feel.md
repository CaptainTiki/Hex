# Project Hex - Style and Feel Guide

## Visual Pillars

- Dark negative space
- Glowing positive space
- Soft blue player
- Sharp red enemies
- Chunky digital-organic terrain
- Swirly animated background
- Particles and distortion for impact

## Color Language

- Blue/Cyan/White: player, friendly, safe, active
- Red/Orange/Magenta: enemy, danger, damage
- Yellow/Gold: reward, interactable, alert
- Purple/Black: corruption, deep system, unstable effects

## Shape Language

- Player: rounded, soft, capsule-like, squishy
- Enemies: angular, sharp, crystalline, rigid
- World: chunky grid structure with organic digital dressing

## Room Readability Rules

- Playable platform tops must be clear
- Enemy silhouettes must not blend into the background
- Background effects should stay darker than gameplay elements
- Fog and particles should add mood without covering gameplay
- Glow should enhance readability, not replace it
